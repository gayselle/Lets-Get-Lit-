What is
Let’s Get Lit??

For this year’s Duma Lit Fest, as part of its ongoing initiative to champion the beauty and significance of literature, one of its initiatives is Let’s Get Lit, a school caravan project to be conducted around Dumaguete City. This literary campaign aims to raise awareness of the state of literature in today’s generation as well as amplify iconic local authors who were pillars of shaping, not just the Dumaguete literary scene but as well as the Philippine literature today.

Our Initiatives

The Literary Movement

Explore the different initiatives we have in store for you this March here at Let’s Get Lit.

Educational Program

In every school caravan, we will conduct a fun educational program, opening the dialogue between literary experts and students about the different literary and literacy challenges we face in today’s generation.

Bazaar

We will also organize a bazaar during the school caravan, cultivating a budding community that shares the same passion for literature.

Survey

As we conduct this literary campaign in different schools here in Dumaguete City, we will also conduct a survey among students regarding their literary and literacy education in our Philippine Educational System.
